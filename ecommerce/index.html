Common Questions

Got Questions? We Got Answers

Quick answers before you dive into the guides.

Are these guides really free?
Yes, every guide on this site is completely free to read — no sign-up, no paywall, no hidden cost.
Do I need money to start an online store?
It depends on the model. Dropshipping needs less capital ($150–$600), while traditional e-commerce with inventory needs more ($500+). Our guides break down exact numbers.
Can I start an online store from Pakistan?
Yes, with some extra steps around payment gateways since Shopify Payments isn't available locally. We cover Pakistan-specific workarounds in our guides.
Which guide should I read first?
Start with "E-commerce vs Dropshipping" — it helps you decide which business model actually fits your budget and goals before you invest time or money.
