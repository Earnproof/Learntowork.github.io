How to Build a Free AI Chatbot for Your Website (No Code)

🗓 2026 ⏱ 8 min read 🎯 Beginner friendly 🆓 Free tools only

Do you want a chatbot on your website but think it is too expensive or too technical? Good news: you can build one for free in under 20 minutes. No coding. No paid subscriptions. Just a free account and a simple tool called Chatbase.

→ What You Will Build

A chatbot that sits on your website and answers visitor questions — based entirely on your own content. It works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, even while you sleep. When a visitor asks a question, the chatbot reads its training data (your FAQ, services, prices) and gives an instant answer.

Here is why this matters: most website visitors leave within 30 seconds if they do not find the answer they want. A chatbot keeps them on your page and answers their question immediately — turning a visitor who was about to leave into a potential customer.

✅ What You Need

A free email address. A computer or phone. The tool we use — Chatbase — is 100% free to start. No credit card. No technical skills. Everything happens inside your browser.

1 Prepare Your Chatbot Content

Before you open Chatbase, write the information you want your chatbot to know. This is called training data — it is exactly like giving your chatbot a study guide. Whatever you write here is what it will answer questions about.

Open a simple text document (Notepad, Google Docs, anything) and write information like this:

📝 Example Training Content — Edit and Use ThisBusiness Name: [Your Name or Business Name] What I do / What this website is about: [Describe your service, product, or purpose in 2-3 sentences] Services and Prices: - [Service 1]: [Price or description] - [Service 2]: [Price or description] - [Service 3]: [Price or description] Frequently Asked Questions: Q: How do I order or get started? A: [Your answer] Q: How long does it take? A: [Your answer] Q: Do you offer refunds or revisions? A: [Your answer] Q: How can I contact you? A: [Email / WhatsApp / Social media] Q: Do you work with international clients? A: [Your answer]
⚠️ Important Rule

Only write information that is accurate and true. Your chatbot will answer visitors based exactly on what you write here. If you write the wrong price, your chatbot will tell visitors the wrong price. Take 5 extra minutes to be accurate — it saves problems later.

2 Create Your Chatbot on Chatbase

1

Create a free account

Go to chatbase.co and sign up with your email. No credit card required. The free plan gives you 1 chatbot and 20 message responses per day — more than enough to start.

2

Click "New Chatbot" and choose Text

Once logged in, click the "New Chatbot" button. You will be asked how to train it. Choose "Text" — this is the fastest option. You will see a large text box where you paste your training content.

3

Paste your content and create the chatbot

Copy everything you wrote in Step 1 and paste it into the text box. Then click "Create Chatbot". Chatbase will read your content and train the AI on it. This takes about 30 seconds.

4

Set the instructions (personality and rules)

Go to your chatbot's settings and find the "Instructions" section. This is where you give the chatbot its personality. Paste this template and edit it:

📋 Instructions TemplateYou are a friendly assistant for [YOUR NAME / BUSINESS NAME]. Answer questions based only on the information you have been given. Always be polite and helpful. If you do not know the answer, say: "I do not have that information. Please contact us at [YOUR EMAIL]." Do not make up answers. Keep replies short — 2 to 3 sentences maximum.
5

Customize the appearance

Go to the "Appearance" tab. Give your chatbot a name (like "Layla's Assistant"), write a welcome message ("Hi! 👋 Ask me anything about our services."), and choose a color that matches your website. This takes 2 minutes and makes it look professional.

💡 Pro Tip

Your welcome message is the first thing visitors see. A specific message like "Hi! Ask me about our design packages, prices, or how to order 🎨" gets far more engagement than a generic "Hello. How can I help?"

3 Test Your Chatbot Before Going Live

On the right side of the Chatbase dashboard, you will see a live preview of your chatbot. Test it like a real visitor would — ask questions your visitors might actually ask, including questions that are NOT in your training data.

For example, if you run a design service, test questions like:

  • "What are your prices?"
  • "How long does an order take?"
  • "Can I get a refund if I'm not happy?"
  • "Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?"
  • "What happens after I place an order?"

If any answer is wrong or unclear, go back to your training text, fix it, and click "Retrain". Test again. Do not skip this step — a chatbot that gives wrong answers destroys trust instantly.

4 Add the Chatbot to Your Website

Go to the "Connect" or "Embed" tab in your Chatbase settings. You will see a small piece of code like this:

💻 Your Embed Code (Example)<script src="https://www.chatbase.co/embed.min.js" chatbotId="YOUR-CHATBOT-ID" domain="www.chatbase.co" defer> </script>

What to do with this code:

  1. Copy the entire code block
  2. Open your website's HTML file
  3. Find the line that says </body> near the bottom of the file
  4. Paste the code on the line just before that closing tag
  5. Save the file and upload it to your server
  6. Open your website — a chat bubble will appear in the bottom right corner
💡 No Website Yet?

No problem. Chatbase gives you a public link like chatbase.co/chatbot/your-id that you can share directly on WhatsApp, Instagram bio, or anywhere online. Use this while you build your website.

→ Why Every Website Needs a Chatbot in 2026

Most small businesses still reply to every customer question manually — by WhatsApp, email, or DM. This takes hours every week and means customers wait hours (or days) for a simple answer like "what are your prices?"

A chatbot fixes this completely. It answers instantly, at any hour, without you doing anything. Customers get their answer. You save your time. And because the chatbot is trained only on your information, every answer is accurate and consistent — no more giving different answers to different customers.

📊 Real Numbers

Websites with live chat (including chatbots) convert 3–5x more visitors into customers compared to websites without. A visitor who gets an instant answer to their question is far more likely to place an order than one who had to send a message and wait.

Building a chatbot on Chatbase takes you 20 minutes. For a business owner with no technical skills, this feels impossible — which means they will happily pay someone to do it for them.

You can offer this service on Fiverr or Upwork for $50–$200 per chatbot. That is $100–$600 per hour equivalent for work that is entirely learnable from this guide. Restaurants, salons, freelancers, online shops — they all have websites and they all have the same problem: customers asking the same questions over and over.
