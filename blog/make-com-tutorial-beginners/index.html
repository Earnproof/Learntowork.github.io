Make.com Tutorial: Automate with ChatGPT for Free

🗓 2026 ⏱ 10 min read 🎯 Beginner friendly 🆓 Free tools only

Do you copy data from one app to another every day? Write the same emails over and over? Update spreadsheets manually? You do not have to. Make.com lets you connect your apps together and add ChatGPT — so everything runs automatically while you focus on real work.

→ What is Make.com?

Make.com (formerly called Integromat) is a visual automation platform. Instead of writing code, you draw a flowchart of what you want to happen. Connect apps, add ChatGPT in the middle, and your workflow runs automatically every time something triggers it.

The free plan gives you 1,000 operations per month — that is enough for dozens of real automations. No credit card needed.

✅ What You Need

A free Make.com account + a free OpenAI API key. Both are free to start. No coding skills needed at all.

→ Build Your First Automation — Step by Step

We will build this: Google Form submitted → ChatGPT writes a reply → saved to Google Sheets. Follow each step in order.

1

Create a free Make.com account

Go to make.com and click "Get started free." Use your email — no credit card. Once logged in, click "Create a new scenario" to open the blank canvas.

2

Add your Trigger — Google Forms

Click the big "+" in the centre of the canvas. Search "Google Forms" and choose "Watch Responses." Connect your Google account and select your form. This tells Make: "Start the workflow whenever someone submits this form."

If you do not have a form yet, create a simple one in Google Forms with a Name and Message field, then come back and select it.

3

Add ChatGPT — OpenAI Module

Click "+" after the Google Forms module. Search "OpenAI" and choose "Create a Completion." Connect your OpenAI API key (get it free at platform.openai.com).

In the Prompt field, write your instruction. Click the coloured bundle tags to insert the actual form data:

📝 Example PromptWrite a short, friendly reply to this message. Person's name: [click Name bundle here] Their message: [click Message bundle here] Keep it to 2-3 sentences. End with: "We will get back to you within 24 hours."
4

Save to Google Sheets

Add another module. Search "Google Sheets" → choose "Add a Row." Create a spreadsheet with columns: Name, Message, AI Reply, Date. Then map the data bundles to each column:

→ Name column → form Name bundle
→ Message column → form Message bundle
→ AI Reply column → OpenAI "choices → message → content" bundle
→ Date column → type {{now}}

5

Test and Activate

Click "Run once" at the bottom left. Submit a test response in your Google Form. Come back to Make — green circles mean success. Open your Google Sheet and check the new row appeared with the ChatGPT reply.

If everything looks correct, click the ON/OFF toggle to activate your scenario. It will now run automatically every 15 minutes — forever.

💡 Pro Tip — Add Gmail Too

Add one more module after Google Sheets: search "Gmail" → "Send an Email." Map the person's email from the form bundle, and the ChatGPT reply as the email body. Now every form submission gets an automatic personalised reply in their inbox within 15 minutes.
